Darkest Dungeon

What is

Darkest Dungeon is a challenging gothic roguelike turn-based RPG about the psychological stresses of adventuring. Recruit, train, and lead a team of flawed heroes against unimaginable horrors, stress, disease, and the ever-encroaching dark. Can you keep your heroes together when all hope is lost?

History

You, as a descendant of an ancient lineage, inherit a castle haunted by an evil force. To restore order, you recruit a team of heroes, each with their own abilities, weaknesses, and personalities, to explore monster-infested dungeons and face the horrors that lurk in the shadows.

Heroes

Cruzader

Battle-hardened and stalwart, the Crusader has held the front lines in a hundred holy wars.

Grave Robber

The Grave Robber is a versatile and nimble combatant, moving back and forth through the ranks with ease.

Jester

The Jester shines in the flickering light of the campfire, bringing his lute and unerring wit to bear against the fears and stress his companions suffer.

Plague Doctor

The Plague Doctor prefers to blight her foes with a deadly concoction of poison dwindling their health away.
